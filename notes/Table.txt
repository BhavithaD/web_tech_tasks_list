Table--> Combination of Rows and columns(Paired tag)
why table in HTML-->To arrange  the content in UI(User Interface)
table is made up of cells



			Structure of Table

<table>// to start table
	<thead>
		<tr>
			<th></th>//table heading
		</tr>
	</thead>
	<tbody>
		<tr>
			<td></td>//table rows
		</tr>
	</tbody>
</table>

>cellpadding(padding) --space b/w content on cell border and cell edge(default is 0px)

>cellspacing(border-spacing) --space b/w each cells(default is 2px)

>border-collapse: collapse; --to merge the table,table head(th),td (default-->border-collapse: separate;)

>to merge columns (colspan)(default-->1)if you want to occupy the next column space

>to merge rows (rowspan)
